About Kea-Med College

Pioneering Medical Education for Over Three Decades

Our History

KEA-MED College, established in Addis Ababa in 2001/02 G.C with 100 nursing students, is a private, low-profit institution dedicated to training highly skilled and ethical nurses capable of providing comprehensive healthcare services. Founded by W/ro Mahider Ziku, whose vision was shaped by her commitment to improving Ethiopia’s health situation, the college grew from very limited resources into a respected training center.

The college is known for its quality education and strong ethical standards, recognized by government hospital professionals. With affordable tuition, KEA-MED aims to improve gender equality, reduce the country’s high mortality and morbidity rates, and positively impact the lives of individuals and communities. Expanding its reach, KEA-MED opened in regional city like bahir dar, debre birhan, debre markos, jimma and plans to extend its services to more underserved and remote areas in Ethiopia.
