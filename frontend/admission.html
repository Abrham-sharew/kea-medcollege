General Requirements

  • Complete Grade 12 or have a prior TVET certificate.
  • Collect your academic documents.
  • Payment of any required application or tuition fee is often required.
  • Institutions allow entry if you have a previous diploma or accreditation.
  • Prepare a valid ID (national ID, school ID, or other official document).
  • Fill out the application form provided by the TVET college.
